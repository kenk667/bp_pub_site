Who We Are

We're here to proliferate modern, high-quality, and secure software development throughout the Naval Enterprise by providing technologies, services, and methodologies that allow more of your team's time and energy to be focused on end user value delivery. We'll show your team how to shorten your capability delivery cycle through DevSecOps Culture, Agile and Lean Methodologies, CI/CD Automation, Lighthouse & Party Barge, Software Modernization, and Accelerated Cyber Accreditation.

Products

Day 0 operation is the hardest and most expensive part of digital transformation. We have products and services designed to accelerate your team and enable you to being your focus back to where it needs to be: rapidly delivering war wining capability across all domains & all missions

Party Barge

The Party Barge is a Naval DevSecOps environment delivered as Software as a Service. Development teams can onboard today and immediately develop and deploy software within a robust and compliant software pipeline.

Lighthouse

Lighouse is ths foundational software platofrm for the Naval Enterprise. Lighthouse provides the Infrastrucure as Code (IaC) to deploy a rock solid PaaS in the cloud, in the data center, or in an operational environment.

Iron Bank

In collaboration with Platform One, Black Pearl contributes to and leverages secure containers from the Iron Bank. Iron Bank provides a secure baseline on which to build your software systems.

DEVSECOPS TRANSFORMATION

Shift Left

We're here to engage your team and walk you through what it takes to go from waterfall to agile. From team management and composition to production support and beyond, we're ready to help you enable your team to go fast by learning how to fail quickly and responsibly early-on.

  • Who's it for?: Everyone!
  • Duration: 3 mo ~ 6 mo

Agile Development

Go Fast Forever

There a lot of questions regarding what it's like to be an effective industry dev team that consistently and continually delivery products on time and on budget. This engagement is meant to not only answer those questions, but show your team how to rise to the productivity level of industry teams.

  • Who is this for?: Developers, Dev team PM
  • Duration: 3 mo to 6 mo

Lean Management

Leadership mentoring from inception to production

A hurdle that most teams encounter is the frozen middle that affect the velocity of effective teams. This engagement is designed to help provide every level of leadership with mentorship that spans management methodologies, skill sets, and everything in-between to best understand how to lead effective and agile teams

  • Who is it for?: PMs, leadership, stakeholders
  • Duration: 3 mo to 6 mo

Application Modernization

Refactor, Greenfield, & Sunsetting

The difficult hurdle every existing product face is understanding what it takes to modernize an application to work with today's software architecture and design patterns. Between technical debt, domain specificity, and mission needs we understand the balance of maintaining mission needs while improving customer experience and security posture. Our team of application transformation (AppTX) experts are ready to work with your team to modernize your application

  • Who is it for?: Dev teams, Product owners
  • Client: 3 mo to 1 yr

Prototype

Accelerate your idea from concept to production

Everyone has amazing ideas, but sometimes we have ideas with the ability to create them. We have various team members spanning a wide range of skills from design to development and are ready to work with you to prototype your idea.

  • Who is it for?: Everyone!
  • Duration: 6 mo to 9 mo

Continuous Authority to Operate

Continuously ship from Dev to Prod by removing friction points

We love going fast and writing new apps, but we recognize the need for an ATO to field our new app. We've changed the way that an ATO is thought of in the Department of the Navy with key authorizing officials and stakeholders. We've also been contributing to the Office of the Secretary of Defense and his team on the continous ATO (c-ATO) playbook. Our Naval-centric c-ATO implementation within Black Pearl is a Naval first and available for your team to consume as we continue to develop it with the DoN

  • Who is it for?: Security, Dev, and Fielding teams
  • Duration: Continuous
